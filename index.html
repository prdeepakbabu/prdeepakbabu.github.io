About Me

Deepak is a Principal AI Scientist with close to two decades of experience in ML/AI, building and shipping large-scale machine learning and deep learning systems across domains including retail, FMCG, search relevance, computational advertising, and voice assistants. He has led technical efforts to improve speech recognition and language understanding at Amazon Alexa and helped build contextual advertising at Amazon Ads. At Alexa, he built personalized, self-healing error-correction foundation models that learn from daily user interactions—enabling run-time query rewriting with speculative execution for low-latency, high-throughput inference on accelerated hardware. His technical leadership bridges research and production, driving early adoption of cutting-edge advances in LLMs, multimodal LLMs, RAG, agents, and cognitive architectures. He holds patents and has published in the areas of speech, language, and information retrieval. He actively contributes to the research community as a reviewer for NeurIPS, ICML, KDD, AMLC, ARA, and EMNLP, and is a Senior Member of IEEE (Seattle Chapter).

Deepak is recognized for first-principles thinking and a constant-learning mindset—qualities that have consistently led to innovative and unconventional solutions to challenging problems. He has also advised early-stage startups in advertising and healthcare. Outside of work, he enjoys reading non-fiction, hiking, landscape/astro photography, and spending time with family.

Past Life: Management consultant for a Fortune-50 CPG/pharma, advising one of the world’s largest loyalty program rollouts.
