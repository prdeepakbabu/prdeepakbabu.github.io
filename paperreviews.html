AI co-scientist Towards an AI co-scientist Artificial Intelligence AI co-scientist, multi-agent system, biomedical discovery Juraj Gottweis, Wei-Hung Weng, Alexander Daryin, Tao Tu, Anil Palepu, et al. Google Cloud AI Research, Google Research, Google DeepMind, Houston Methodist, Sequome, Fleming Initiative and Imperial College London, Stanford University School of Medicine This paper introduces an AI co-scientist, a multi-agent system built on Gemini 2.0, designed to uncover new knowledge and formulate novel research hypotheses in biomedicine. The system employs a generate, debate, and evolve approach inspired by the scientific method, focusing on drug repurposing, novel target discovery, and explaining mechanisms of bacterial evolution and antimicrobial resistance.
